NutriSavings Landing Pages

Design, Development

When I first started working at NutriSavings, it was immediately apparent to me that the landing pages needed some serious revamping. So I took it upon myself to make it more aesthetically pleasing as well as functional. They were all hosted on Silverpop. In a nutshell, I added a logo and navigation section at the top; increased the sizes of various elements (title and download imagery mostly); included some on-brand color and background imagery to give the pages more intrigue; reformatted the entire space to make more visual sense with information hierarchy in mind; refined the contact form fields to better drive lead conversion; and finally added an extra contact section at the bottom.
