SGX Warfighter Patch

Graphic Design

Spartan required many print materials to be created to be handed out or set up on site at the races. One such material I was able to work on was a patch to hand out to finishers at certain military-affiliated races. The mandatories of the project were to include the name "SGX Warfighter" and to utilize the military brand colors. I had creative freedom over everything else. I wanted to keep with the military theme, which led me to designing within this badge shape. The military has many symbols associated with different branches, rankings, etc. So I wanted to keep the design elements of the patch simple so as not to offend.
