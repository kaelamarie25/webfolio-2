Emails

Design, Development

As part of my role at NutriSavings, I designed, developed, tested and scheduled a large portion of our emails. Though it's not one of my favorite email marketing platforms, Silverpop is what we used to manage our emails and corresponding landing pages. We were a small company; we sent out about 15–20 emails a month. There was one for the monthly newsletter, at least one main marketing material to promote, and several other smaller promotional marketing emails.
