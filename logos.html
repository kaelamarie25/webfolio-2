Logos

Graphic Design

I love logos. They're quicker projects usually, and can be a great way to exercise creativity in a simple and manageable way. I like to look around daily life to find logos that are great inspo, or would be fun to redesign. I also like to think up random company names and position statements or slogans and design a logo around that concept.
